The Monk Seal

A Poem

I am a seal
which lacks a true deal
In heaven I rest
waiting for god's test


Like a good catholic should
in morn' I eat wood
Transcribing numerous books
and giving children looks!


I am a good pious man
for I do what I can
To spread god's dream on Earth
and sleep on a beach in Perth.
